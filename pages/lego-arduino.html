hacking lego motors with arduino

See how you can hack your lego motors and control them via the Arduino IDE program.



I had the need to create a reliable and easy way to connect Lego Motors to Arduino. This project used:
  • Arduino UNO R3
  • NPN (BJT) Transistor
  • Connector Pins
  • Breadboard Wires
  • LEGO Motor
I knew that the 5V that the Arduino outputed wasn't resonable for the motors, so I designed a circuit utilising the Transistor to output 9V when voltage is being passed through the Base.

The ouput is connected to the C1 pin of the LEGO motor and the negative connects to the GND of the 9V battery (see circuit diagram above).
